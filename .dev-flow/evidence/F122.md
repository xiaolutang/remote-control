## 架构模式

```
_PanelStateFields (mixin on State<...>)
  ├── ~40 字段抽象 getter/setter 声明
  ├── derived getters: _isConnected, _isAgentActive, _isPhaseActive
  └── 跨 mixin 方法签名（~30 个）

_PanelStateLogicMixin on _PanelStateFields
  └── _resetAgentRenderState, _applyConversationProjection, _deriveAgentRenderState, ...

_PanelHandlersMixin on _PanelStateFields
  └── _handleResolveIntent, _startAgentSession, _handleAgentEvent, ...

_PanelConversationMixin on _PanelStateFields
  └── _buildAgentBody, _buildCollapsibleHistoryEntry, ...

_PanelResultViewsMixin on _PanelStateFields
  └── _buildProgressView, _buildResultView, _buildErrorView, ...

_PanelInputMixin on _PanelStateFields
  └── _buildInputBar, _buildAskingView, _buildUserBubble, ...

_PanelWidgetsMixin on _PanelStateFields
  └── _buildAssistantBubble, _buildLoadingBubble, ...
```

State 类 `with` 链: `_PanelStateFields, _PanelStateLogicMixin, _PanelHandlersMixin, _PanelConversationMixin, _PanelResultViewsMixin, _PanelInputMixin, _PanelWidgetsMixin`

所有跨 mixin 方法调用通过 `_PanelStateFields` 的抽象声明桥接，运行时由 State 类的 mixin 组合提供具体实现。
