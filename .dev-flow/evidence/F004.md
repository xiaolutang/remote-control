1. **TerminalPageIndicator at IndexedStack outer layer**: Same position as CompactTabStrip (Column child after Expanded(IndexedStack)), not in bottomChrome slot

3. **Controller data assertion for title verification**: TerminalPageIndicator displays page count only, not terminal titles; tests verify titles via `controller.terminals.first.title`
